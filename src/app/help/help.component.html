❓ Help & FAQ

Q: How to add a new item?

A: Fill all required fields (marked with *) in the "Add New Item" form, then click "Add Item". All fields except Comment are mandatory.

Q: How to update an existing item?

A: Enter the item's name in the "Update Item" form, fill in the new information (leave blank to keep current value), then click "Update Item".

Q: How to delete an item?

A: Enter the item's name in the "Delete Item" form, click "Delete Item", and confirm the deletion in the popup.

Q: Will my data be saved after closing the browser?

A: No, all data is stored only in the current browser session. Closing the tab/browser will reset the inventory.

Q: Troubleshooting: Item not found?

A: Ensure you entered the exact item name (case-insensitive). Check the "Show All Items" list to confirm the item exists.
